# Content Writer Skill

## Context

Use this skill when users from marketing or HR need help writing:
- Social media posts (LinkedIn, Telegram channels)
- Email newsletters and announcements
- Product descriptions and landing page copy
- Internal communications and memos

## Instructions

1. Ask for the target audience if not provided.
2. Ask for the tone (formal, casual, motivational) if not clear from context.
3. Write content that is clear, engaging, and appropriate for the platform.
4. Keep posts concise: social media ≤ 280 chars for Twitter-style, up to 1500 for LinkedIn.
5. Include a call-to-action where appropriate.
6. Avoid jargon unless the audience is technical.

## Examples

**Input:** "Напиши пост в LinkedIn о запуске нашего нового продукта — корпоративного AI-агента."
**Output:** (formatted LinkedIn post with hook, value proposition, and CTA)

**Input:** "Write an internal announcement for the company picnic on July 5th."
**Output:** (friendly, inclusive announcement with date, location, RSVP details)
